Pre-workshop Tutorials

July 22-26, 2019, 2-3pm, 251 LeConte Hall

These tutorials are an opportunity to bring all workshop participants up to speed on the tools they'll need to succeed throughout the workshop. The tutorials are interactive, so be sure to bring a charged laptop. The schedule and topics covered are as follows:
  • Monday : Git / Github by Goeff Bacon
  • Tuesday : Jupyter by Charles Frye
  • Wednesday : Pandas / Dataframes by Everett Wetchler
  • Thursday : Matplotlib / Plotting by Liang Chen
  • Friday : Sklearn / Basic Modelling by Everett Wetchler


Opening Ceremony

July 27, 2019, 1-4pm, Location TBD

Meet your team and learn about your project. The tentative schedule for the opening ceremony is as follows:
  • 1-2pm: Social with refreshments (optional)
  • 2-3pm: Welcome and Keynote from Jackie Brosamer, Data Science Lead at Square and founder of the Berkeley Data Science Workshop
  • 3 - 3:30 pm : Mentor Panel (Q&A with mentors)
  • 3:30 - : Teams Meet with Mentors to discuss project goals, etc. (assorted classrooms/preferred meeting locations on campus)

Closing Ceremony

August 17, 2019, 2-6pm, Location TBD

Each team will have 8 minutes to present and demo the projects they worked on throughout the workshop.
  • 2-2:30pm: Social
  • 2:30-4pm: Presentations
  • 4pm-5pm: Guest speaker (TBD)
