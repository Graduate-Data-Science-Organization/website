We are a graduate student organization at UC Berkeley geared towards providing graduate students and postdocs with resources to explore careers in data science.

We are a new organization, so this site is still a work in progress. Get in touch with us or join our e-mail list to get the latest updates about the events we have in the works.

We are a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this site. GDSO is sponsored by ASUC. Logo design based on original photo from Schreiner's Iris Gardens.
