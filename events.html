Events

The GDSO provides opportunities for graduate students to gain skills and explore careers in data science through the annual Data Science Workshop, tutorials, project hack nights, speaker events, and site visits with Bay Area tech companies employing data scientists.

Subscribe to our email list to find out about these events as they're scheduled!

Monthly Meetups

In order to facilitate new connections on campus, we’ll be hosting a series of monthly meetings. We welcome all who are interested in data science to come join us. These meetings will be informal and feature a few short talks about a variety of topics relevant to our organization. The first meeting will be Wednesday, January 17 at 5pm at the Berkeley Institute for Data Science (190 Doe Library). We’ll have three members of our founding team give short talks about how they use data science in their research.

Starting a Career in Data Science

Our first event was a panel discussion and networking mixer with former academics who have successfully transitioned into data science careers.

Speakers
  • Caroline Sofiatti (Noon Home)
  • Lenny Evans (Uber)
