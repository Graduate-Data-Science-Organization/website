Events

Most of the leadership of the organization has participated or planned the Data Science Workshop that was previously organized through the Career Development Initiative in the Physical Sciences (CDIPS). The GDSO seeks to expand this program with tutorials, project hack nights, speaker events, and site visits with Bay Area tech companies employing data scientists.

Subscribe to our email list to find out about these events as they're scheduled!

We are a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this site. GDSO is sponsored by ASUC. Logo design based on original photo from Schreiner's Iris Gardens.
