FoodFlix

Mentor: Hyesoo Yoon
Participants: Kyle Bilton, Shudan Zhong, Javier Caravaca, Sudha Subramanian

Our team developed a food recommendation web app from the ground up, from scraping data off of the internet, all the way to an app written in Flask and deployed on Heroku. The recommendation system, which is based on ingredients within recipes, returns recommendations to users based on their preferences (i.e., dishes they liked or disliked), as well as dietary restrictions. The performance of the recommendation system was evaluated against a system that randomly returns recipes, and our system was shown to significantly outperform random guesses.

The project can be seen in action at foodflix-api-heroku.herokuapp.com. Please see http://jcaravaca.github.io/foodflix/ and http://github.com/jcaravaca/foodflix for more information.
